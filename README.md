# Evergreen Adventure 🌲

A premium-quality mobile open-world cartoon life adventure game built for web browsers with optimal iPhone support.

## Game Features

### Open World
- **Evergreen Valley** - A large connected world with 8 unique regions
- **Seamless Exploration** - No loading screens, complete world exists together
- **Dynamic Weather** - Changing seasons and weather patterns
- **Living NPCs** - 100+ characters with daily routines, relationships, and personalities

### Career System
- **15+ Career Paths** including:
  - Emergency Services (Police Officer, Firefighter, Park Ranger, Coast Guard, Lifeguard)
  - Transportation (Taxi Driver, Bus Driver, Pilot, Delivery Driver)
  - Business (Restaurant Worker, Mechanic, Farmer)
  - Creative (Photographer, Journalist, Wildlife Researcher)
- **Career Progression** - Advance from entry-level to leadership positions
- **Career Missions** - Unique gameplay for each profession
- **Equipment & Vehicles** - Specialized gear for different careers

### Housing & Customization
- **6+ Home Types** - Cabins, apartments, houses, farms, beach homes, mountain homes
- **Home Decoration** - Customize furniture and decorations
- **Property Management** - Upgrade and expand your homes

### Vehicles
- **8 Vehicle Types** - Bicycles, motorcycles, cars, trucks, boats, jet skis, helicopters, planes
- **Vehicle Customization** - Upgrade and maintain your fleet
- **Transportation** - Fast travel across the world

### Smartphone System
- **Contacts App** - Manage friendships and relationships
- **Messages App** - Receive missions and invitations
- **Banking App** - Manage finances and accounts
- **Job Search App** - Browse and apply for careers
- **GPS App** - Navigate the world and find locations
- **Camera App** - Take photos for photography career

### Activities & Entertainment
- **Fishing** - Catch fish in various locations
- **Camping** - Explore and camp in the wilderness
- **Photography** - Capture nature and sell photos
- **Farming** - Grow crops and raise animals
- **Sports** - Bowling, arcade games, surfing, skiing
- **Treasure Hunting** - Discover hidden items and secrets

### Relationship System
- **NPC Relationships** - Build friendships with 100+ characters
- **Dialogue Choices** - Shape relationships through conversations
- **Gift Giving** - Give items to improve relationships
- **Events & Hangouts** - Spend time with friends

## Game Regions

1. **Evergreen Forest** - Hiking, camping, wildlife, ranger missions
2. **Crystal Lake** - Fishing, boating, water sports, islands
3. **Pine Ridge Mountains** - Skiing, climbing, mining, mountain rescue
4. **Sunny Shores Coast** - Surfing, lifeguarding, diving, boat tours
5. **Evergreen City** - Jobs, shopping, entertainment, urban life
6. **Maplewood Town** - Small community, local businesses, charm
7. **Golden Fields Farm** - Farming, animals, harvest events, country life
8. **Harbor Island** - Resort, rare wildlife, treasure, exploration

## How to Play

### Getting Started
1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Explore the world and meet NPCs
3. Apply for a career using the smartphone
4. Complete missions and earn money
5. Buy a home and vehicles
6. Build relationships with NPCs
7. Discover secrets and hidden locations

### Controls
- **Arrow Keys / WASD** - Move around
- **E / Enter** - Interact with NPCs and buildings
- **Phone Button** - Open smartphone interface (bottom right)
- **Touch** - All interactions work on touchscreen
